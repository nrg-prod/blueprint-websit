History

Founded in 2005 by Mr Raju K. Ramasamy, Blueprint Nusantara Indonesia has grown rapidly and established itself internationally. We have adopted the best practices of financial advice through our  Blueprint Nusantara Indonesia partnership with Blueprint Wealth Australia, which has been consistently ranked as one of the highest performing financial advisories in Australia. With a view to expanding across the ASEAN region, we have recently opened an office in Jakarta and aim to open our Surabaya office in 2016.

 

Our Structure

Within the Blueprint group there are two companies:

  • Blueprint Nusantara Indonesia Sdn. Bhd.
  • Blueprint International Sdn. Bhd.

Blueprint Nusantara Indonesia focuses on all your financial planning needs and is licensed by the Securities Commission Malaysia. Blueprint International (BPI) is focused on delivering insurance based solutions from a range of leading providers. BPI is licensed to advice and provide insurance products approved by Bank Negara Malaysia local or offshore.
