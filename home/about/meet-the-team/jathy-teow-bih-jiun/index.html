Jathy Teow Bih Jiun
Case Management Executive

Jathy is as a Case Manager with the Blueprint Planning team. She holds a Bachelor of Financial Planning from INTI International University and the qualification of Registered Financial Planner (RFP). Prior to joining Blueprint, Jathy gained considerable management experience through her time working at the Royal Bank of Canada. Currently, she assisting Licensed Financial Planner processing Financial Plan documentation, maintains all transactions records and responding to all servicing enquiries from clients.
